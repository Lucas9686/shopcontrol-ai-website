**Status:** SHIPPED 2026-01-31
**Phases:** 1-4
**Total Plans:** 8

## Overview

Bilingual presentation website for the ShopControl AI diploma thesis at the Jugend Innovativ competition. Built with Next.js 16 + shadcn/ui + Tailwind v4 + Framer Motion. Covers problem, solution, technology deep-dive, results/financials, about, and legal pages in DE and EN with dark blue SaaS theme, scroll animations, SEO metadata, and demo video embed.
